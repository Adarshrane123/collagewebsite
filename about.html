About Our College

Discover the rich history and values that shape our institution

Our History

Vivekananda Polytechnic Sitasaongi was established with a vision to provide quality technical education to students from rural and semi-urban areas. Founded in the spirit of Swami Vivekananda's teachings, our institution has been a beacon of knowledge and innovation.

Over the years, we have grown from a small technical institute to a comprehensive polytechnic college offering multiple engineering disciplines. Our commitment to excellence and student success has remained unwavering since our inception.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical education that empowers students with practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and ethical values necessary for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institution recognized for excellence in technical education, innovation, and producing skilled professionals who contribute significantly to society and industry.
